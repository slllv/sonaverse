The Sonaverse

The sonaverse is a liminal space where all virtual characters are meant to reside. As the Sonaverse grows, more new exciting features will be added, in order to allow creators and characters to interact within this space! We will start by adding recognized original characters from independent creators.. but as the site grows, we plan on adding authentication features and character submission forms, as well as contests and games.



Contact

You can contact us to add new characters, ask questions or make promotion inquiries. we will give you a reply as soon as we can! (Feel free to resend your mail if you get no response after a week!)


Support

Sonaverse is a passion project, but as everything, it has costs associated with it. These include basic maintenance, updates and hosting/domain costs.If you believe in this project, a small tip is really appreciated! Other ways you can support us are promoting your personal characters, and following SLLLV on social media!

This website is always a work a progress. feel free to contact us to make the Sonaverse a good place to stay fo everyone!
